Read the rules





  1. Click on the Play button to determine the first player
  2. The first player chooses their token (danfo bus or Keke Napep tricycle)
  3. Click on the Play button.
  4. Move the tokens from GO according to the dice score.
  5. If you roll doubles go back to GO.
  6. If you get to the end, you have to play the exact score that will take you back to Go or else remain at the same spot until you get that score.
  7. The first person to get back to Go wins the game.
  8. Have fun by Touring Naija. NO DULLING! SHINE YOUR EYE WELL WELL!!
